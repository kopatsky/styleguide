Does any external landscape feature (trees, poles or street furniture) obscure CCTV views of the area immediately beyond your perimeter?

Answer is: Yes

Comments: There are trees that are obscuring the western corner wall camera.

Advice: The ability to view your perimeter from a security standpoint enables potentially early warning of a physical attack on your perimeter. Where the attack is an active shooter or knife attack this warning may make the difference between being able to lock down your building to prevent access by the attackers thereby reducing the risk of death or injury to staff and or visitors and the attackers getting inside where the likelihood of mass casualties is much greater.

Risk

There are external landscape features (trees, poles or street furniture) that obscure CCTV views of the area immediately beyond your perimeter?

Mitigation Plan

Prune the trees so that the corner wall camera has full view of the access road to the west and east.
